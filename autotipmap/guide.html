Guide

Everything you need to know about using Auto Tip Map.

Importing Data
Google Maps Import

You can import your delivery history from Google Maps using Google Takeout. Here's how:

  1. Open your web browser and go to Google Takeout.
  2. Scroll through the list and find "Maps." Make sure its checkbox is checked and it says "1 selected" at the top. If other items are checked, uncheck them — exporting extra data will take much longer. Tap "Next step."
  3. Make sure "Send download link via email" and "Export once" are selected. Tap "Create export."
  4. Google will prepare your file. This is usually almost instant, but can sometimes take a little while. You'll receive an email when it's ready.
  5. Open the email titled "Your Google data is ready to download." Tap the link and download the .zip file to your phone. It's usually named something like takeout-maps.zip.
  6. Return to Auto Tip Map and go to Settings > Data Management > Import Data. Select "Google Maps Backup" and choose the .zip file you downloaded.
Backup Import

If you've previously exported a backup from Auto Tip Map, you can restore it by going to Settings > Data Management > Import Data, then select "Auto Tip Map Backup" and choose your .atm backup file. This will import all of your saved addresses, orders, and tip data.

Screenshot Import

Screenshot import is coming soon. This feature will allow you to import order and tip data from screenshots.

Backing Up Data

To back up your data, go to Settings > Data Management and tap "Export Backup." This will save all of your addresses, orders, tips, and other data to a .atm file on your device. You can use this file to restore your data later or transfer it to another device.

Automatic Data Capture
Tip Capture

Auto Tip Map can automatically capture tips in two ways:

  • Overlay Capture — When the overlay is running, Auto Tip Map detects tips in real time and saves them automatically.
  • Background Capture — When enabled in Settings, Auto Tip Map listens for tip notifications from your delivery app even when the app is closed. Any tips that come through as notifications are automatically saved. You can enable or disable this in Settings > App Behavior.
Order Capture

When the overlay is running, Auto Tip Map automatically detects delivery orders in real time. When a new order is found, the app saves the order number, address, and other details. You'll see a brief confirmation popup when an order is captured.

Map
Heatmap

When you zoom out on the map, nearby addresses are grouped together into colored bubbles. Each bubble's color represents the average tip rating for addresses in that area. Colors range from red (no tips) through orange and yellow to green (great tips). This gives you a quick visual overview of which areas tend to tip well.

Pins

Each address on your map is shown as a colored pin. You can tap any pin to see a popup with the address details, including how many times you've delivered there and the average tip. Tap the address in the popup to open the full order history for that location.

Overlay
Order Notifications

When the overlay is running and a new order is detected, a brief popup appears near the overlay bubble confirming that the order has been saved. This notification fades away automatically after a couple of seconds.

Tip Notifications

When a tip is detected and logged — either through the overlay or background capture — a brief popup appears confirming that the tip has been saved. This notification fades away automatically after a couple of seconds.

Offer Screen

When the overlay detects a delivery offer on your screen, it shows a popup with information about the address from your order history. This helps you decide whether to accept the offer. The popup will show one of the following:

  • New Customer — the address has never been seen before
  • No Order History — the address is known but has no past orders
  • Order history details — number of past orders, average tip amount and percentage, and a color-coded tip rating (No Tip, Poor, Average, Good, or Great)

If you've added notes to an address, a sticky note icon will appear. Tap it to view your notes.

The popup is draggable — tap and hold to move it anywhere on your screen. It appears automatically when an offer is detected and disappears when you leave the offer screen. If multiple addresses are visible, all of them will be shown.

Capture Paused

If the overlay is interrupted (for example, if your screen locks), capture will pause. The overlay button on the home screen will show "Paused" to let you know. You can resume capture by unlocking your screen and tapping the resume prompt, or by restarting the overlay from the home screen.

Time Tracking
Enabling / Disabling

Time Tracking is an optional feature. To turn it on or off, go to Settings > App Behavior and use the Time Tracking toggle.

When enabled, a time tracking button appears on the home screen, the Time Tracking page becomes available in the sidebar menu, and the Hours and Hourly Rate cards appear on the Stats page. When disabled, all of these features are hidden.

Starting / Stopping

You can start tracking your work time from the home screen by tapping "Start Time Tracking." While tracking, the button changes to "Stop Time Tracking" and shows how long you've been working. Tap it again to stop. Time Tracking must be enabled in Settings > App Behavior to see this button.

Manually Adding Entries

To manually add a work shift, open the Time Tracking page from the sidebar menu and tap "+ Add Shift." Enter the start and end times for your shift.

Editing Entries

To edit an existing time entry, go to the Time Tracking page and tap on the shift you want to change. You can adjust the start and end times.

Deleting Entries

To delete a time entry, go to the Time Tracking page, tap on the shift you want to remove, and select the delete option.

Order History
Sorting & Filtering

In Order History, you can filter your addresses by date range using the buttons near the top of the page: Today, Yesterday, 7 Days, or Custom (which lets you choose your own start and end dates).

You can also toggle between Order Date and Tip Date using the toggle at the top. Order Date filters by when the order was placed, while Tip Date filters by when the tip was received.

To sort your addresses, use the sort button in the search bar. Sort options include:

  • Tip Rating — sort by how well each address tips
  • Most Recent — sort by the date of the most recent order
  • Last Updated — sort by when the address was last modified

You can also toggle between ascending and descending order using the arrow button next to the sort menu.

Tip Rating

Each address is assigned a color-coded tip rating:

Great Good Average Poor No Tip

The rating is calculated based on your tip threshold settings. You can customize how tip ratings are calculated by going to Settings > Data Preferences, where you can choose between percentage-based or dollar-based thresholds and adjust the cutoff values for each tier.

Adding Addresses

You can manually add a new address by tapping the green "+" button at the top of the Order History page. However, manual address entry is not recommended because manually entered addresses may not match correctly with automatically captured orders. For best results, let Auto Tip Map capture addresses automatically through the overlay or through your uploaded screenshots.

Adding Orders

To manually add an order to an existing address, open the address card in Order History and use the add order option. You can enter the order number, order amount, and tip amount.

Editing Orders

To edit an order, open the address card in Order History and tap on the order you want to change. You can update the order details such as the order number, amount, and tip.

Editing Addresses

Addresses cannot be directly edited. This is because changing an address can cause issues with automatic order matching — Auto Tip Map may not be able to correctly link future orders to a modified address.

Stats
Date Range Filters

The Stats page lets you view your earnings data for different time periods. Use the date range buttons at the top to switch between:

  • Today — stats for the current day
  • Yesterday — stats for the previous day
  • 7 Days — stats for the past week
  • Custom — choose your own start and end dates
Orders Completed

This shows the total number of deliveries you completed during the selected time period. Tap this card to jump to Order History filtered to the same date range.

Tips for Today's Orders

This shows the total dollar amount of tips you received for orders placed during the selected time period. The label changes based on your selected date range (e.g., "Tips for Yesterday's Orders").

% of Orders Tipped

This shows the percentage of your orders in the selected time period that received a tip.

Bonus Total

This shows the total amount of bonuses you've added for the selected time period.

Hours

This shows the total hours you've tracked during the selected time period. This card is only visible when Time Tracking is enabled in Settings > App Behavior. Tap it to go to the Time Tracking page.

Hourly Rate

This shows your total earnings divided by your tracked hours for the selected time period. This helps you see how much you're earning per hour. This card is only visible when Time Tracking is enabled.

Past Tips Received Today

This shows tips that were received during the selected time period but belong to orders from earlier dates. For example, if you delivered an order yesterday but the tip came through today, it would show up here. Tap this card for more details.

Bonuses

The Bonuses section at the bottom of the Stats page lets you track extra earnings like peak pay, challenges, or other bonuses. Tap "+ Add Bonus" to log a new bonus with an amount and date. You can tap an existing bonus to edit or delete it.

Settings
Appearance

App Theme controls the overall look of the app. You can choose System (follows your phone's setting), Light, or Dark.

Overlay Theme controls the appearance of the overlay popup that appears over other apps. You can choose Light, Dark, or System. Light is recommended since most delivery apps use a light background.

Data Preferences

Tip Counting Preference lets you choose how tips are counted in your stats:

  • Order Date — tips are counted based on when the order was placed
  • Tip Date — tips are counted based on when the tip was received

Tip Rating Thresholds let you customize what counts as a "poor," "average," "good," or "great" tip. You can choose between Percentage (based on tip as a percent of order amount) or Dollar Amount (based on the raw tip value). Drag the sliders to adjust the cutoff for each tier.

Tap "Save & Recalculate" to apply your changes and update all existing tip ratings across your data. Tap "Reset to Defaults" to restore the original threshold values.

App Behavior

Background Tip Capture: When enabled, Auto Tip Map listens for tip notifications from your delivery app even when the app is closed. This requires notification access permission. If disabled, tips will only be captured while the overlay is running.

Time Tracking: When enabled, a time tracking button appears on the home screen and the Time Tracking page becomes available in the sidebar menu. When disabled, these features are hidden.

Data Management

Export Backup saves all of your data to a .atm backup file on your device. Use this regularly to keep a safe copy of your data.

Import Data lets you bring in data from a Google Maps export, a previous Auto Tip Map backup, or screenshots.

Delete All Data permanently erases all of your addresses, orders, tips, and other saved data. This action cannot be undone.
