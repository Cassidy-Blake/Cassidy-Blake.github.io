The Problem

Shipt shoppers have no way to know if a customer tips well before accepting an order. Many resort to manually labeling addresses in Google Maps and matching tip notifications days later.

✅

The Solution

Auto Tip Map runs quietly in the background, automatically saving orders and tips. When a new offer comes in, it instantly shows you that customer's tip history.

How It Works

Auto Tip Map works automatically in the background while you deliver. Here's the flow:

1

Complete an Order

Just deliver like you normally would. When you complete an order, Auto Tip Map automatically saves the address, order number, and pay amount for you.

2

Order Gets Logged Automatically

You'll see a quick "Order logged" message pop up on your screen so you know it worked.

3

Tips Are Captured & Matched

When you get a tip, Auto Tip Map sees the notification and connects it to the right order. This works even when you've closed the app, so you never miss a tip.

4

See Tip History on New Offers

Next time you get an offer to that same address, a popup instantly appears on your screen showing you whether this customer tips and how well, rated from Great to No Tip.

Features

Everything you need to track tips and make better delivery decisions.

Works While You Drive

Auto Tip Map automatically saves your orders and tips as you deliver. Everything stays on your phone, nothing is sent anywhere.

Never Miss a Tip

Even when you close the app, Auto Tip Map keeps watching for tip notifications and logs them for you automatically.

Order History

See all your past deliveries in one place, organized by address. Quickly search for any customer and see their full tip history at a glance.

Earnings Stats

See how much you're making at a glance. Track your daily earnings, tips, hours worked, and what you're earning per hour.

Bring Your Existing Data

Already tracking tips with Google Maps labels or screenshots? Import everything you've already saved so you don't have to start from scratch.

Secure Backups

Save a backup of all your data and move it to a new phone anytime. Your backup is password-protected so only you can access it.
