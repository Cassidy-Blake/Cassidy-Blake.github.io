The Problem

Shoppers have no way to know if a customer tips well before accepting an order. Many resort to juggling Google Maps labels and matching tip notifications days later — all while trying to stay focused on the road.

✅

The Solution

Auto Tip Map runs quietly in the background, automatically saving orders and tips. When a new offer comes in, it instantly shows you that customer's tip history — no switching apps, no looking anything up.

How It Works

Auto Tip Map works automatically in the background while you deliver.

Rest easy — Auto Tip Map doesn't connect to Shipt's servers, access your Shipt account, or modify the Shipt app in any way. It simply reads order information already shown on your screen and saves everything locally on your phone — an easier version of the tip tracking many shoppers already do by hand.

Here's the flow:

1

Complete an Order

Just deliver like you normally would. When you complete an order, Auto Tip Map automatically saves the address, order number, and pay amount for you.

2

Order Gets Logged Automatically

You'll see a quick "Order logged" message pop up on your screen so you know it worked.

3

Tips Are Captured & Matched

When you get a tip, Auto Tip Map sees the notification and connects it to the right order. This works even when you've closed the app, so you never miss a tip.

4

See Tip History on New Offers

Next time you get an offer to that same address, a popup instantly appears on your screen showing you whether this customer tips and how well, rated from Great to No Tip.
